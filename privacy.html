Privacy Policy

Effective 19 July 2026

Kinly is built for small, private circles of friends — not for advertising or data resale. This page explains, in plain language, what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

Who this covers

Kinly is developed by an independent developer (contact below), not a registered company. This policy applies to the Kinly mobile app (Android and iOS) and the Kinly website (github.io/kinly). It does not cover third-party services you separately link to Kinly, such as Google, whose own privacy policy governs how they handle your data.

What we collect

Kinly is an accountability app for small friend groups ("Growth Circles"), so most of what we store is content you and your circle create on purpose — goals, check-ins, messages to each other. Here's the full picture:

CategoryWhatWhy
Account Email, password (or Google account info if you use "Continue with Google"), name To create and secure your account
Profile Bio, avatar (an uploaded photo, or a generated cartoon avatar) Shown to the friends in your circle
Circle & goals Circle membership, invite codes, goal titles/targets/progress, streaks, interests you select The core of the app — tracking progress with your circle
Circle content Ask Friends posts and replies, nudges, Vision Board images, Meet Up plans, poll/game answers Visible only to your circle, never public
Future Letters Private notes you write to your future self, time-locked for a year Visible only to you — not even your circle can read these
Notifications A device push-notification token To deliver nudges and reminders from your circle
Diagnostics Crash and error reports, if this is enabled in a given app version To find and fix bugs

We do not collect your precise location, contacts list, browsing history, or advertising identifiers. We have no ads and no analytics/tracking SDKs in the app.

AI features

A few features — nudge message suggestions, a weekly circle recap, and a circle activity insight — are generated by sending a summary of relevant circle activity (like goal titles and completion counts, not your private messages) to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the text. This happens on our server, never directly from your device, and Anthropic does not use this data to train their models under our agreement with them.

Who we share data with

We don't sell your data, and we don't share it with advertisers. A few service providers process data on our behalf, strictly to run the app:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage (avatars, Vision Board images).
  • Anthropic — generates the AI features described above, from circle activity summaries only.
  • Expo — delivers push notifications and app updates.
  • Google — only if you choose "Continue with Google" to sign in.
  • DiceBear — generates cartoon avatars from a random seed, if you choose one instead of uploading a photo; no personal data is sent.

We'll only disclose your data beyond this if required by law, or to protect the safety of Kinly's users.

Your circle can see your content

Kinly is built around small private circles, not public sharing — but within your circle, other members can see your goals, progress, posts, and replies by design. Future Letters are the one exception: those are private to you alone. If you leave a circle, your past activity in that circle isn't automatically erased for other members, the same way a group chat doesn't erase your old messages when you leave it.

How long we keep data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete a goal, a post, your profile, or your account, we mark it as removed immediately so it disappears from the app and from your circle — the underlying record is retained briefly for backup and abuse-prevention purposes, then permanently erased.

Your choices

  • Access or correct your data — edit your profile, goals, and posts directly in the app at any time.
  • Delete content — goals, posts, and Vision Board images can be deleted from within the app.
  • Delete your account — email us (below) and we'll delete your account and personal data within 30 days. We're working on making this self-service directly in the app.
  • Notifications — mute categories per-circle in Circle Settings, or disable push notifications entirely in your device settings.

Children

Kinly is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the effective date above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app.
